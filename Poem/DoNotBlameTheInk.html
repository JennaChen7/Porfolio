POEM
DO Not Blame The Ink
Jenna Chen
September 11, 2020
"Blame the ink
for leaving no trace on the dull piece of paper
I shall not."



墨怨

暮里成醉欲成诗，屏倒烛折人愁怨。

夕雨悲风乱窗纱，无奈情思难落墨。

Will anyone speak from the heart
when they get drunk?
I don't know.
I did.
Rain of a summer night,
Wind of a pale autumn,
window covered in ashes,
floor stained by candle wax.
It's sad,
But never enough
if to compare with my unrequited emotions.
Blame the ink
for leaving no trace on the dull piece of paper
I shall not.
I know this for sure.
